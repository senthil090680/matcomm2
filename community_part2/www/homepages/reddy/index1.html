The Celebrating Reddy:
Our community has the rare distinction of producing several chief ministers of Andhra Pradesh. This is not surprising considering our long tradition of being village headmen and Zamindhars in Andhra Pradesh. We have influential leaders from our community in national politics too.

Wedding customs

The customs and traditions of our community are usually similar to those followed by other communities in Andhra Pradesh.
Patrika
This is the written marriage contract between the two families. The families of the bride as well as groom consult their respective astrologers and fix the date of marriage. The marriage contract is placed on an yellow cloth along with fruits, betel nuts, betel leaf and turmeric. The priest chants mantras while the fathers of the bride and groom exchange the Patrika.
